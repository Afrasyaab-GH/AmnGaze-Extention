17. CHROME WEB STORE PERMISSION JUSTIFICATIONS
===========================================

contentSettings Permission Justification:
Amn Shield uses the contentSettings permission to enforce Safe Search on search engines (Google, Bing, Yahoo, DuckDuckGo) as part of our faith-aligned protection features. This ensures users who enable Safe Search in settings have it consistently applied across all supported search engines, helping maintain a halal browsing environment. This is a core privacy and protection feature requested by Muslim families and students to prevent exposure to inappropriate content in search results.

alarms Permission Justification:
Amn Shield uses the alarms permission to provide accurate Islamic prayer time notifications and reminders. The extension calculates and schedules alarms for the five daily prayers (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha) based on the user's location and selected calculation method. These scheduled reminders help Muslims maintain their spiritual obligations throughout their browsing day. Additionally, alarms are used to refresh prayer time calculations daily and manage timed reminder features for adhkaar (Islamic supplications).
